Privacy Policy

Last updated June 26, 2026

This policy covers the Narrows mobile app. Looking for the policy that covers narrows.site and our waitlist signup? See our website privacy policy instead.

This Privacy Policy explains how Narrows ("we", "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Narrows mobile application (the "App"). Narrows is a boating and fishing community app for the Simcoe County, Ontario area.

By creating an account or using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

1. Who we are

Narrows is based in Orillia, Ontario, Canada. For any privacy question or request, contact us at support@narrows.site.

2. Information we collect

Account information

  • Email address and password (password is stored hashed by our auth provider).
  • First and last name.

Profile / onboarding information

  • Region, boat ownership, and experience level you provide during onboarding.
  • Optional profile photo.

Content you create

  • Posts (trips, catches, check-ins), captions, photos you upload.
  • Likes and comments.
  • Catch pins, fishing spots, and trip records, including location coordinates.

Location information

  • With your permission, we collect your device's GPS location to show your position on the map, let you drop catch pins, and record trips.
  • You can disable location access at any time in your device settings (some features will not work without it).

Usage and device information

  • Basic technical data needed to run the App (e.g., app version, device type, error logs).

We do not intentionally collect financial information or government IDs.

3. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the App (accounts, feed, map, weather, trips).
  • To display your name and content to other users (see "What's public" below).
  • To improve features and fix problems.
  • To communicate with you about your account (e.g., email confirmation, password reset).
  • To select and display advertising based on the onboarding information you provide (e.g., region, boat ownership, experience). See Section 6.

4. What is public vs. private

  • Public to other signed-in users: your first/last name, profile photo, posts, captions, photos, likes, comments, and (where applicable) catch/trip locations you choose to share.
  • Private: your email address and password are never shown to other users.

Do not post anything you are not comfortable making visible to the community.

5. How we share information — service providers

We use trusted third parties to run the App. They process data only to provide their service to us:

  • Supabase — database, authentication, and file storage (hosts your account and content).
  • Resend — sending account emails (e.g., confirmation and password-reset codes).
  • Apple Maps — displaying maps.
  • Open-Meteo — weather data (we send only marina coordinates, not your personal information).

We may add other weather/radar data providers (such as RainViewer) in the future. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Advertising

We may show advertising in the App. Where we use your onboarding information (such as region or boat ownership) to make ads more relevant, we will disclose this and, where required by law, offer a way to opt out. We do not share your identity (name/email) with advertisers.

7. Location data — specifics

  • Location is used for: showing your position, catch pins, suggested spots, and trip tracking.
  • Location is collected only while you use the relevant features (foreground).
  • Catch/spot/trip locations you save are stored in our database; you control whether you create them, and you can delete them.

8. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your profile and associated content are deleted (some data may remain in backups for a limited period, and we may retain limited records where required by law).

9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law (including Canada's PIPEDA), you may:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and personal information (see Section 10).
  • Withdraw consent (e.g., turn off location), understanding some features may stop working.

To make a request, email support@narrows.site.

10. Deleting your account

You can delete your account by emailing support@narrows.site from the email address on your account. Deleting your account removes your profile and content (posts, comments, likes, catch pins, trips) via cascading deletion.

11. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under that age. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact support@narrows.site and we will delete it.

12. Security

We protect your data using industry-standard measures, including Row Level Security on our database (so users can only access data they are permitted to). No system is perfectly secure, but we work to safeguard your information.

13. International processing

Our service providers may process and store data outside your province or country (e.g., in the United States). By using the App you consent to this processing.

14. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy. We will post the new "Last updated" date and, for material changes, notify you in the App or by email.

15. Contact

Narrows — support@narrows.site — Orillia, Ontario, Canada
